Foie Gras Ice Cream

Ingredients:

    Fresh Foie Gras 450g
    Milk 400ml
    Sodium Alginate 4.5g
    Aged Brandy 2.5ml
    Black Pepper. fresh ground, 1g
    Salt 2g

Method:

  1. Deveign the foie gras (see how) and cut into one 300g portion and one 150g portion.
    (D'Artagnan is a good place to get foie gras)
  2. Cut the 150g portion into 1/2" cubes and place in a plastic bag to be put into a hot water bath.
  3. In a water bath, warm the sealed 150g foie gras to 40℃ and then pass through a fine mesh sieve.
  4. In a very hot sautee pan, sear the 300g foie gras until very dark. Drain any excess fat.
  5. In a saucepan add the milk, brandy and pepper. Heat to 60℃ and let steep.
  6. In a blender, combine the milk mixture and foie gras paste.
  7. Slowly add the sodium alginate, then the seared foie gras.
  8. Pass the mixture through a fine mesh sieve.
  9. Run in your home ice cream maker.
  10. Serve with torn pieces of the Savory Peppercorn Sponge Cake or as desired.
