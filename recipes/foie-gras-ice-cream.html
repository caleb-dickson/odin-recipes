Foie Gras Ice Cream

Ingredients:

    Fresh Foie Gras 900g
    Milk 800ml
    Sodium Alginate 9g
    Aged Brandy 5ml
    Black Pepper. fresh ground, 2g
    Salt 5g

Method:

  1. Deveign (Watch) the foie gras and cut into one 600g portion and one 300g portion.
  2. Cut the 300g portion into 1/2" cubes and place in a plastic bag to be put into a hot water bath.
  3. In a water bath, warm the sealed 300g foie gras to 40 deg Celsius and then pass through a fine mesh sieve.
  4. In a very hot sautee pan, sear the 600g foie gras until very dark. Drain any excess fat.
  5. In a saucepan add the milk, brandy and pepper. Heat to 60 deg Celsius and let steep.
  6. In a blender, combine the milk mixture and foie gras paste.
  7. Slowly add the sodium alginate, then the seared foie gras.
  8. Pass the mixture through a fine mesh sieve.
  9. Run in your home ice cream maker.
  10. Serve with torn pieces of the Savory Peppercorn Sponge Cake or as desired.
