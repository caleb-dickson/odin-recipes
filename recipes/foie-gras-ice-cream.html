Foie Gras Ice Cream

Ingredients:

    Fresh Foie Gras 900g
    Milk 800ml
    Sodium Alginate 9g
    Aged Brandy 5ml
    Black Pepper. fresh ground, 2g
    Salt 5g

Method:

    Deveign (Watch) the foie gras and cut into one 600g portion and one 300g portion.
    Cut the 300g portion into 1/2" cubes and place in a plastic bag to be put into a hot water bath.
    In a water bath, warm the sealed 300g foie gras to 40 deg Celsius and then pass through a fine mesh sieve.
    In a very hot sautee pan, sear the 600g foie gras until very dark. Drain any excess fat.
    In a saucepan add the milk, brandy and pepper. Heat to 60 deg Celsius and let steep.
    In a blender, combine the milk mixture and foie gras paste.
    Slowly add the sodium alginate, then the seared foie gras.
    Pass the mixture through a fine mesh sieve.
    Run in your home ice cream maker.
    Serve with torn pieces of the Savory Peppercorn Sponge Cake or as desired.
